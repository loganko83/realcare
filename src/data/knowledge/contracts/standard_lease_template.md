# Standard Lease Contract Template (표준 임대차 계약서)

## Overview
This document describes the standard lease contract format recommended by the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport.

## Essential Contract Elements

### 1. Property Information (부동산 표시)

Required information:
- Full address (도로명 주소)
- Building type (건물 유형)
- Floor and unit number (층/호)
- Exclusive area (전용면적)
- Shared area (공용면적)
- Land area (if applicable)

### 2. Lease Terms (임대차 조건)

**Deposit and Rent:**
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Jeonse | Full deposit, no monthly rent |
| Monthly | Deposit + monthly rent |
| Mixed | Larger deposit, reduced rent |

**Contract Period:**
- Minimum recommended: 2 years
- Maximum: No legal limit (but 20 years common max)
- Renewal rights: Available under Housing Lease Protection Act

### 3. Standard Clauses

#### Deposit Clauses (보증금 관련)

**Standard Language:**
"The tenant shall pay the deposit as follows:
- Down payment: XX% upon contract signing
- Interim payment: XX% by [date]
- Balance: XX% on move-in day"

**Key Points:**
- Deposit must be returned within 1 month of lease end
- Interest on late return: Legal rate applies
- Deductions require mutual agreement

#### Maintenance Clauses (수선 관련)

**Landlord Responsibility:**
- Major structural repairs
- HVAC system failures
- Plumbing main lines
- Electrical main systems
- Waterproofing

**Tenant Responsibility:**
- Minor repairs under 300,000 KRW
- Consumable items (lightbulbs, filters)
- Damage caused by tenant

#### Early Termination (중도 해지)

**Standard Terms:**
- 2 months advance notice required
- Penalty: Usually 10-20% of deposit
- No penalty if landlord sells property

### 4. Special Conditions (특약사항)

Common special conditions to include:
1. Interior condition at move-in
2. Existing fixtures and furniture
3. Utility meter readings
4. Pet policy
5. Renovation restrictions
6. Subletting policy

## Warning Signs in Contracts

### High-Risk Clauses to Avoid

**1. Excessive Penalties**
- Risk: "50% deposit forfeiture for early termination"
- Standard: Should not exceed 10-20%
- Legal basis: Civil Act Article 398 (penalty reduction)

**2. Unlimited Landlord Entry**
- Risk: "Landlord may enter at any time"
- Standard: 24-48 hours advance notice required
- Exception: Emergency repairs only

**3. Delayed Deposit Return**
- Risk: "Deposit returned within 60 days"
- Standard: Within 1 month, ideally 1-2 weeks
- Legal: Tenant may deduct from last payment

**4. One-sided Termination**
- Risk: "Landlord may terminate with 1 month notice"
- Standard: 6 months notice required by law
- Protection: Housing Lease Protection Act

**5. Waiver of Rights**
- Risk: "Tenant waives all rights under Housing Lease Protection Act"
- Status: Void - cannot waive legal protections
- Action: Remove clause entirely

## Checklist Before Signing

### Document Verification
- [ ] Landlord ID matches property registration
- [ ] No outstanding mortgages exceeding deposit
- [ ] Building permits are in order
- [ ] No pending legal disputes

### Property Inspection
- [ ] All utilities functioning
- [ ] No water damage or mold
- [ ] Locks and security working
- [ ] Documented current condition with photos

### Contract Review
- [ ] All amounts match verbal agreement
- [ ] Dates are specific and reasonable
- [ ] Special conditions documented
- [ ] Both parties have signed copies
