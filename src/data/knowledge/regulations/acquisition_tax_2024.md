# Acquisition Tax Regulations 2024

## Overview
Last Updated: 2024-01-01
Source: Ministry of Economy and Finance

## Basic Acquisition Tax Structure

Acquisition tax consists of three components:
1. Acquisition Tax (취득세)
2. Local Education Tax (지방교육세)
3. Rural Special Tax (농어촌특별세) - for properties over 85 sqm

## Tax Rates by Housing Count

### Single Home Buyers (1주택자)

| Property Value | Base Rate | With Local Education | Total Effective |
|---------------|-----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Under 600M KRW | 1.0% | +0.1% | 1.1% |
| 600M - 900M KRW | 2.0% | +0.2% | 2.2% |
| Over 900M KRW | 3.0% | +0.3% | 3.3-3.5% |

Additional 0.2% Rural Special Tax for areas over 85 sqm.

### Two Home Buyers (2주택자)

**In Regulated Areas:**
| Tax Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Acquisition Tax | 8.0% |
| Local Education Tax | 0.4% |
| Total | 8.4% |

**In Non-regulated Areas:**
| Tax Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Acquisition Tax | 1-3% |
| Local Education Tax | 0.1-0.3% |
| Total | 1.1-3.5% |

### Three or More Homes (3주택자 이상)

**In Regulated Areas:**
| Tax Type | Rate |
|----------|------|
| Acquisition Tax | 12.0% |
| Local Education Tax | 0.4% |
| Total | 12.4% |

## First-time Home Buyer Reduction

Eligible buyers may receive:
- 50% reduction on acquisition tax
- Property value limit: 600M KRW
- Income limit: 70M KRW annually

## Calculation Examples

### Example 1: First Home in Seoul (800M KRW)
- Base Rate: 2.0% (600-900M tier)
- Acquisition Tax: 16M KRW
- Local Education Tax: 3.2M KRW (20% of acquisition)
- Rural Special Tax: 0 (if under 85 sqm)
- **Total: 19.2M KRW (2.4% effective)**

### Example 2: Second Home in Gangnam (1.5B KRW)
- Base Rate: 8.0% (multi-house in regulated area)
- Acquisition Tax: 120M KRW
- Local Education Tax: 6M KRW
- **Total: 126M KRW (8.4% effective)**

### Example 3: Third Home (1B KRW)
- Base Rate: 12.0%
- Acquisition Tax: 120M KRW
- Local Education Tax: 4M KRW
- **Total: 124M KRW (12.4% effective)**

## Tax Payment Timeline

| Event | Deadline |
|-------|----------|
| Contract signing | N/A |
| Registration | Within 60 days of acquisition |
| Tax payment | Before registration |
| Late penalty | 3% per month |

## Exemptions and Reductions

### Full Exemption
- Properties under 10M KRW
- Inheritance with registration transfer only

### Reduced Rates
- Newlywed couples: Up to 50% reduction
- Multi-child families: Up to 50% reduction
- Relocation from overseas: Varies

## Special Considerations

### Regulated Area Changes
When an area is newly designated as regulated:
- Properties acquired before designation: Original rates apply
- Properties acquired after: Higher rates apply

### Gift vs Purchase
Gift acquisitions have different rates:
- Direct family gifts: Lower rates available
- Non-family gifts: Standard rates apply
