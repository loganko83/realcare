# LTV/DSR Regulations 2024

## Overview
Last Updated: 2024-01-15
Source: Financial Services Commission (FSC)

## LTV (Loan-to-Value) Limits

### Definition
LTV = (Mortgage Amount / Property Value) x 100

The maximum loan amount is determined by the LTV limit applicable to the borrower and property location.

### Speculative Overheated Districts (투기과열지구)
Strictest regulations apply.

| Buyer Type | LTV Limit |
|------------|-----------|
| First-time home buyer | 50% |
| 1 house owner | 30% |
| 2+ house owners | 0% (No mortgage) |

Applicable Areas (as of 2024):
- Seoul: All 25 districts
- Gyeonggi: Gwacheon-si, Seongnam-si (Bundang-gu, Sujeong-gu)
- Incheon: Yeonsu-gu, Nam-gu, Seo-gu (partial)

### Adjusted Areas (조정대상지역)
Moderate regulations apply.

| Buyer Type | LTV Limit |
|------------|-----------|
| First-time home buyer | 60% |
| 1 house owner | 50% |
| 2+ house owners | 30% |

### Non-regulated Areas
Standard limits apply.

| Buyer Type | LTV Limit |
|------------|-----------|
| All buyers | 70% |

## DSR (Debt Service Ratio) Limits

### Definition
DSR = (Annual Debt Payments / Annual Income) x 100

All principal and interest payments for all loans are included in the calculation.

### Standard DSR Limit: 40%

Applies to:
- All mortgage loans in regulated areas
- Loans exceeding 100 million KRW

### Calculation Components

**Included in DSR:**
1. New mortgage principal and interest
2. Existing mortgage payments
3. Credit card minimum payments
4. Personal loan payments
5. Car loan payments
6. Student loan payments (if applicable)

### DSR Exceptions

| Loan Type | DSR Treatment |
|-----------|---------------|
| Jeonse loans under 300M | Simplified calculation |
| Loans under 100M | DTI-based (not DSR) |
| Policy mortgage products | Up to 60% allowed |

### First-time Home Buyer Benefits

Buyers meeting the following criteria may qualify for relaxed limits:
1. No history of home ownership
2. Annual income under 70M KRW
3. Property price under 600M KRW

Available benefits:
- LTV: Up to 70% (regardless of zone)
- DSR: Up to 60%
- Reduced acquisition tax

## Practical Examples

### Example 1: First-time Buyer in Gangnam
- Property: 1.2B KRW
- Income: 80M KRW/year
- Location: Gangnam-gu (Speculative zone)

Calculation:
- LTV Limit: 50% (speculative zone, first-time)
- Max Loan by LTV: 600M KRW
- DSR Limit: 40%
- Max Annual Payment: 32M KRW
- Max Loan by DSR (at 4.5%, 30yr): ~520M KRW
- **Actual Max Loan: 520M KRW (DSR limited)**

### Example 2: 1-house Owner in Mapo
- Property: 800M KRW
- Income: 60M KRW/year
- Location: Mapo-gu (Adjusted zone)

Calculation:
- LTV Limit: 50% (adjusted zone, 1-house)
- Max Loan by LTV: 400M KRW
- DSR Limit: 40%
- Max Annual Payment: 24M KRW
- Max Loan by DSR: ~390M KRW
- **Actual Max Loan: 390M KRW (DSR limited)**
